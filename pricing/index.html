Common Questions

Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. For projects over $1,000 we typically split into 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For larger projects we can arrange milestone-based payments.
How long does a project take?
Simple automations and landing pages: 5–7 days. AI chatbots and business websites: 10–14 days. Full AI apps and complex workflows: 3–6 weeks depending on scope.
What if I need something not listed here?
Just reach out. These packages cover the most common needs but we build custom solutions regularly. If you can describe it, we can likely build it.
What does the monthly retainer include?
Retainers cover ongoing maintenance, updates, monitoring, and priority support. You also get a 15% discount vs. one-time pricing, and we treat retainer clients as long-term partners with faster turnaround times.
Do I own everything you build?
Absolutely. Once the project is paid in full, all code, designs, and assets are yours. No licensing fees, no lock-in.
